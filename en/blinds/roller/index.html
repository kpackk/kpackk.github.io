Roller Blinds: A Practical Solution for Dubai

Roller blinds are among the most popular sun protection systems in the UAE. Their popularity stems from an ideal combination of functionality, aesthetics, and affordable pricing. The mechanism is simple and reliable: a fabric panel wraps around an aluminum tube mounted at the top of the window frame, operated by a chain, spring mechanism, or electric motor.

For residential spaces in Dubai, we recommend fabrics with a light-blocking coefficient of 70% or higher. Sun-filtering screen fabrics with a perforated structure preserve panoramic views of the city or sea while blocking UV radiation and reducing indoor temperatures. For bedrooms, blackout fabrics with full light opacity and a special heat-reflective coating on the reverse side are the optimal choice.

Cassette roller blinds represent an enhanced design where the tube is concealed within a decorative housing and the fabric travels along side channels. This system eliminates light gaps along the edges and ensures a tighter fit against the window. This is especially important for panoramic windows in high-rise buildings where sunlight enters at varying angles.

Solara Design manufactures roller blinds of any size, tailored to each window opening. Our specialist will visit for a free measurement, help select the ideal fabric and mounting type.
