Vertical Blinds: Flexibility and Elegance

Vertical blinds remain one of the most sought-after window treatment solutions for commercial and residential properties in Dubai. The design consists of vertically arranged vanes attached to an aluminum headrail via carriers. The vanes can slide left, right, or split from the center, providing maximum flexibility in use.

The key advantage of vertical blinds over other sun protection systems is the ability to simultaneously control two parameters: the amount of incoming light and the degree of privacy. By rotating the vanes, you create soft diffused lighting without direct sunlight. By fully drawing the blinds, you free the window opening for maximum natural light.

In Dubai's office environments, vertical blinds solve the problem of screen glare while maintaining natural lighting for workstations. For meeting rooms and executive offices, we recommend dense fabric vanes that ensure complete confidentiality during business meetings.

For residential spaces, designer vane collections with natural wood, linen, and silk textures are available. A special anti-static treatment prevents dust buildup, significantly simplifying blind maintenance. Vertical Blinds FAQ

Helpful information for making your choice

Vertical blinds are ideal for wide window openings, panoramic glazing, and sliding balcony doors. The vanes slide to the side without obstructing passage. We manufacture vertical blinds up to 6 meters wide for large-scale glazing in Dubai villas and penthouses.
Vanes are made from specialized fabrics of varying density: from semi-transparent to fully light-blocking. PVC vanes are also available for high-humidity areas. Standard vane widths are 89 mm or 127 mm — narrow ones suit smaller windows, while wider vanes are best for panoramic glazing.
Fabric vanes only need wiping with a damp cloth or vacuuming once a month with a soft brush attachment. PVC vanes can be washed with plain water. We use anti-static materials that attract less dust — especially important in Dubai's climate with frequent sandstorms.
