About Solara Design

Solara Design is a team of professionals specializing in bespoke window treatment solutions in Dubai. We offer a full cycle of services: from consultation and measurement to production and installation of curtains, blinds, and motorized systems.

Over the years, we have completed hundreds of projects in residential complexes, villas, and office spaces across Dubai. Our experience allows us to find the optimal solution for any interior and budget, taking into account the specifics of the UAE's hot climate and the need for effective sun protection.

We partner with leading fabric suppliers from Europe and Asia, ensuring high material quality and UV resistance. Every project receives meticulous attention to detail, precise measurement, and flawless installation, backed by a 2-year warranty on all products and workmanship.
