Roman Shades — Where Style Meets Practicality

Roman shades are one of the oldest and most elegant types of window treatment, and they remain highly relevant in modern Dubai interiors. The principle is straightforward: the fabric panel lifts upward via a cord or chain mechanism, folding into neat horizontal pleats. When lowered, the fabric creates a smooth, flat surface; when raised, it forms a graceful cascade of folds.

The key advantage of Roman shades is their compact footprint. Unlike traditional drapes, they require no space on either side of the window and do not protrude into the room. This makes them the perfect choice for kitchens, bathrooms, nurseries, and any space where every centimetre counts. They can be mounted inside the window recess or on the wall above — both options look clean and polished.

At Solara Design, we offer Roman shades in a wide variety of fabrics: from lightweight linen and cotton to dense blackout materials.
