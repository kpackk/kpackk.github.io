Curtains for Your Home in Dubai

Window treatments play a key role in creating a comfortable living space, especially in Dubai's hot climate. Properly selected curtains not only enhance your interior but also protect against the blazing sun, reduce the load on your air conditioning, and ensure privacy in your home.

At Solara Design, we offer a full range of custom curtains: from lightweight sheer fabrics that gently diffuse daylight, to dense blackout curtains that create total darkness in the bedroom. Day-night curtains allow flexible light control throughout the day, while Roman shades combine practicality with refined design.

We work exclusively with trusted fabrics that resist UV exposure and fading. Our collection features natural materials such as linen, cotton, and silk, as well as modern technical fabrics with sun-protective and heat-insulating properties. All fabrics are certified and adapted for UAE conditions.

The ordering process is as convenient as possible. Our specialist will visit you free of charge, take precise window measurements, and help you select the fabric, color, and style that complement your interior. After approval, we produce your curtains in 4-5 business days and install the rods with full fitting. We serve all of Dubai: from Downtown and Dubai Marina to JBR, Palm Jumeirah, and residential communities in newer districts.

All products come with a 2-year warranty. We stand behind the quality of our materials and workmanship, guaranteeing durability and an impeccable look throughout the entire lifespan of your curtains.
