Why Choose Custom-Made Curtains

Ready-made curtains from a store are always a compromise. Standard sizes rarely match your actual window dimensions, fabric choices are limited, and tailoring quality often falls short. Custom tailoring at Solara Design solves all these issues: you get curtains designed specifically for your home.

The process starts with a free visit from our specialist. The measurement designer arrives at a time that suits you, precisely measures every window, and advises on fabric selection. We bring samples to your home so you can see the material under your actual lighting conditions — eliminating any unpleasant surprises after production.

Our catalogue features over 500 fabrics for every purpose. For living rooms — elegant velvet drapes and weightless sheers. For bedrooms — dense blackout for complete darkness. For kitchens — practical moisture-resistant materials. For nurseries — hypoallergenic certified fabrics. All textiles are tested for UV resistance and heat tolerance — a crucial requirement for Dubai.

Tailoring is done in our own atelier within 4-5 working days. The order price includes everything: fabric, hardware, curtain rod, delivery, and professional installation. We provide a 2-year warranty on all products and installation work. If desired, curtains can be fitted with a motorised track for smartphone control.
