Sheer Curtains for Your Dubai Home

Sheer curtains are the foundation of window decor, creating an atmosphere of lightness and airiness in any room. In Dubai's hot climate, quality sheers serve multiple functions: they diffuse intense sunlight, provide privacy from outside views, and protect furniture and textiles from UV-induced fading.

At Solara Design, we offer a wide selection of sheer fabrics: from weightless organza with a subtle sheen to soft voile with a pleasant matte texture. For modern minimalist interiors, mesh sheers with a clean weave are an excellent choice, while lace and jacquard options with intricate patterns suit classic living rooms beautifully.

Every order begins with a free visit from our measurement specialist. Our expert will bring fabric samples, help you choose the right shade and density based on your window orientation and the lighting conditions of your apartment or villa. We account for ceiling height, curtain rod width, and your preferred mounting style — grommets, loops, tape, or hooks.

Tailoring is done in our Dubai atelier within 4-5 working days. All fabrics are certified, resistant to frequent washing, and suited to the UAE's climate. The order price includes not only tailoring but also curtain rod installation — no need to find a separate contractor. The result is perfectly fitted sheers that transform your space and last for years.
