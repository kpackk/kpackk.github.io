Japanese Panels — Minimalism and Functionality

Japanese panels (also called Japanese curtains) are sliding fabric panels mounted on a multi-track ceiling rail. The design is inspired by traditional Japanese shoji screens and fits seamlessly into modern minimalist interiors. Each panel, 60 to 120 cm wide, glides along its own track like a sliding door, creating a perfectly smooth surface with no folds or drapes.

Japanese panels are the ideal solution for the panoramic glazing that Dubai's skyscrapers and apartments are known for. Floor-to-ceiling windows in towers across Dubai Marina, Downtown, and Business Bay require a special approach to window dressing, and sliding panels handle the task flawlessly. Beyond window decor, panels are widely used as room dividers — they can replace permanent partitions, separating a work area from a lounge zone or a walk-in wardrobe from a bedroom.

We offer a wide range of fabrics for Japanese panels: from semi-transparent materials that gently diffuse light to dense blackout options. All panels are compatible with Somfy motorised tracks, enabling control via smartphone, remote, or smart home system. The Velcro mounting system makes fabric replacement quick and simple — you can update your interior design in minutes without removing the track.
