How to Choose Office Blinds in Dubai

Office spaces in Dubai face a unique set of challenges: intense sun creates glare on monitors, heats up the workspace, and puts extra load on air conditioning systems. At the same time, window treatments need to look professional, be easy to operate, and comply with fire safety regulations. In this guide, we'll walk through the main types of office blinds and help you choose the best option for your business.

Vertical Blinds

Vertical blinds are the classic solution for offices with large windows. Wide slats (89 mm or 127 mm) effectively block sunlight while allowing precise light control by rotating to any angle. Vertical blinds are especially practical for wide window openings -- a single unit can cover windows up to 6 metres across.

The main advantage of vertical blinds is ease of maintenance. Individual slats are easily removed for cleaning or replacement. If one slat gets damaged, you don't need to replace the entire system -- a significant benefit in high-traffic offices. Pricing starts from 100 AED per square metre, making them one of the most budget-friendly options for outfitting a large office space.

The downside: vertical blinds can create a distinctly "corporate" look that doesn't always suit design-forward interiors. For executive offices and meeting rooms, consider more refined alternatives.

Roller Blinds

Roller blinds are the top choice for meeting rooms, conference halls, and open-plan spaces. The smooth, flat fabric creates a neat, contemporary appearance without visual distractions. For offices, solar screen fabric is ideal -- a specialised sun-protection material with micro-perforation that blocks up to 95% of heat and UV radiation while maintaining outward visibility.

Solar screen effectively eliminates glare on computer monitors and projector screens -- an essential quality for presentation rooms. The fabric is non-flammable (fire rating B1/M1), which is important for meeting commercial fire safety codes. Pricing for roller blinds with solar screen fabric starts from 150 AED per square metre.

For executive offices, roller blinds with dimout fabric (80-90% light blocking) or complete blackout are excellent options. A system with side channels ensures the fabric sits flush against the window, eliminating light gaps for maximum privacy and presentation quality.

Horizontal Blinds

Horizontal blinds in aluminium or wood offer an elegant solution for executive offices and reception areas. Aluminium slats (16 mm or 25 mm wide) provide precision light control -- by tilting the slats to the desired angle, you direct light exactly where it's needed.

Wooden horizontal blinds (bamboo, basswood, cedar) create a sense of warmth and prestige. They're ideal for offices with a classic design direction. 50 mm-wide slats look substantial and sophisticated. However, wooden blinds require more attentive care: regular dry cleaning and treatment to prevent drying out in air-conditioned environments.

Aluminium horizontal blinds start from 120 AED/sqm, wooden from 250 AED/sqm. Maximum recommended width for a single unit is 2.5 metres; wider windows use multiple sections.

Motorization for the Office

Automated blind control isn't just about convenience -- it delivers real savings. Programmable schedules can close blinds during peak sun hours (10:00 to 16:00) and open them in the morning and evening. Studies show that automated sun protection reduces air conditioning costs by 15-25%.

For offices with 20 or more windows, motorization is practically a necessity. Manually adjusting dozens of blinds wastes employee time and leads to uneven coverage. Group control systems let you manage every blind on an entire floor with a single button press or tablet command. Integration with BMS (Building Management System) is a standard option for Class A office buildings in Dubai.

Budget Guide

Indicative pricing for office blinds in Dubai (per square metre, 2026): vertical blinds -- from 100 AED, roller blinds (solar screen) -- from 150 AED, roller blinds (blackout) -- from 180 AED, aluminium horizontal -- from 120 AED, wooden horizontal -- from 250 AED. Motorization adds from 350 AED per window (wired) or from 500 AED (battery-powered).
